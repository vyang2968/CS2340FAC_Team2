What is WanderSync?

WanderSync is a collaborative travel management system designed to simplify the creation and organization of travel itineraries for both solo and group travelers. The app allows users to manage locations, transportation, accommodations, dining reservations, and personal notes.

Features

  • Secure account creation.
  • User-friendly itinerary management tools.
  • Real-time collaboration for group travel planning.
  • Integration of travel details like destinations, schedules, lodging, and dining preferences.
  • Shared travel agendas and synchronized updates among group members.
